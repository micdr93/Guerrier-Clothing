RETURNS

At Guerrier Streetwear, we strive to ensure our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a simple returns process for unworn items with tags still attached within 30 days of purchase.

HOW TO RETURN

Returns are free! Just follow the instructions below to return your item at no extra cost.

FAQ

How do I return an order from Guerrier Streetwear?

Guerrier Streetwear customers can easily return their orders by posting them back with a free return label provided by us. For full details, please follow our return instructions included in your order confirmation.

Can I return a defective or faulty item?

If you have received a defective or faulty item, you can return it with valid proof of purchase within two years from the date of purchase.

If you believe your item is faulty and it was delivered within 30 days of purchase, it qualifies for a standard return. If more than 30 days have passed, please contact us to initiate the return process for defective items.
